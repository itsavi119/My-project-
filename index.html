📚 Understanding Your Cycle ▶
🩸 Menstrual · Days 1–5
Uterine lining sheds. Low hormones. Cramps, bloating common.
🌱 Follicular · Days 1–13
Eggs mature. Oestrogen rises. Energy and mood improve.
🥚 Ovulation · Day ~14
Egg released. Most fertile time. Discharge may be clearer.
🌙 Luteal · Days 15–28
Progesterone rises. PMS symptoms may appear near period.
💡 Tracking Tips
  • Enter 2–3 recent cycle lengths for a more accurate date range
  • Normal cycles: 21–35 days · Period: 3–7 days
  • Stress, illness, and weight changes can shift your cycle
  • Track 3+ months to spot patterns
⚠️ See a Doctor If
  • Cycle <21 or >35 days consistently
  • Period lasts >7 days or very heavy flow
  • Severe pain interfering with daily life
  • Missed periods (not pregnant)
  • Bleeding between periods

🫘 eGFR Calculator

Estimated Glomerular Filtration Rate using the CKD-EPI 2021 equation — the gold standard for kidney function assessment and CKD staging per KDIGO guidelines.

🫁 CURB-65 Score

Community-Acquired Pneumonia severity score. Guides inpatient vs outpatient treatment decisions. British Thoracic Society guideline — Lim et al. Thorax 2003.

🦵 Wells DVT Score

Pre-test probability of Deep Vein Thrombosis. Wells et al. Lancet 1997. Guides D-dimer testing and compression ultrasound decisions.

❤️ CHA₂DS₂-VASc Score

Annual stroke risk in non-valvular atrial fibrillation. ACC/AHA 2019 & ESC 2020 guidelines. Guides anticoagulation therapy decisions.

📈 QTc Interval Calculator

Corrected QT interval using Bazett and Fridericia formulas. Identifies prolonged QT risk for drug-induced Torsades de Pointes (TdP).

🎯 Ideal Body Weight

Devine formula (1974) for IBW and Adjusted Body Weight. Essential for drug dosing, ventilator tidal volume settings, and nutritional assessment.

🩺 Blood Pressure Classifier

Classifies blood pressure using ACC/AHA 2017 Hypertension Guidelines. Provides stage, cardiovascular risk, and management recommendations.

💉 Opioid Conversion (MME)

Morphine Milligram Equivalent converter for opioid rotation. Based on CDC 2022 Clinical Practice Guideline for Prescribing Opioids.
